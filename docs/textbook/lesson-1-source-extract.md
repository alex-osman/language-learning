## Lesson Header

- English title: Greetings
- Chinese title: 问好
- Pinyin: Dì yī kè Wèn hǎo

## Learning Objectives

- Exchange basic greetings.
- Request a person's last name and full name and provide your own.
- Determine whether someone is a teacher or a student.
- Ascertain someone's nationality.

## Dialogue I: Exchanging Greetings

1. 你好！
   - Pinyin: Nǐ hǎo!
   - English: Hello!
2. 你好！
   - Pinyin: Nǐ hǎo!
   - English: Hello!
3. 请问，你贵姓？
   - Pinyin: Qǐng wèn, nǐ guì xìng?
   - English: May I ask, what is your surname?
4. 我姓李。你呢？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ xìng Lǐ. Nǐ ne?
   - English: My surname is Li. How about you?
5. 我姓王。李小姐，你叫什么名字？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ xìng Wáng. Lǐ xiǎojiě, nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
   - English: My surname is Wang. Miss Li, what is your name?
6. 我叫李友。王先生，你叫什么名字？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ jiào Lǐ Yǒu. Wáng xiānsheng, nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
   - English: My name is Li You. Mr. Wang, what is your name?
7. 我叫王朋。
   - Pinyin: Wǒ jiào Wáng Péng.
   - English: My name is Wang Peng.

## Dialogue II: Asking about Someone's Nationality

1. 王先生，你是老师吗？
   - Pinyin: Wáng xiānsheng, nǐ shì lǎoshī ma?
   - English: Mr. Wang, are you a teacher?
2. 我不是老师，我是学生。李友，你呢？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ bú shì lǎoshī, wǒ shì xuésheng. Lǐ Yǒu, nǐ ne?
   - English: I am not a teacher. I am a student. Li You, how about you?
3. 我也是学生。你是中国人吗？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ yě shì xuésheng. Nǐ shì Zhōngguó rén ma?
   - English: I am also a student. Are you Chinese?
4. 是，我是北京人。你是美国人吗？
   - Pinyin: Shì, wǒ shì Běijīng rén. Nǐ shì Měiguó rén ma?
   - English: Yes, I am from Beijing. Are you American?
5. 是，我是纽约人。
   - Pinyin: Shì, wǒ shì Niǔyuē rén.
   - English: Yes, I am from New York.

## Tutor Pattern Targets

- Greeting and response: 你好！ 你好！
- Polite inquiry: 请问，你贵姓？
- Surname: 我姓李。
- Follow-up question with `呢`: 你呢？
- Full name: 你叫什么名字？ 我叫王朋。
- Identity with `是`: 我是学生。
- Yes/no question with `吗`: 你是老师吗？
- Negation with `不`: 我不是老师。
- Addition with `也`: 我也是学生。
