## Learning Objectives

- Employ basic kinship terms.

## Dialogue I: Looking at a Family Photo

1. 高文中，那是你的照片吗？
   - Pinyin: Gāo Wénzhōng, nà shì nǐ de zhàopiàn ma?
   - English: Gao Wenzhong, is that picture yours?
2. 是。这是我爸爸，这是我妈妈。
   - Pinyin: Shì. Zhè shì wǒ bàba, zhè shì wǒ māma.
   - English: Yes. This is my dad. This is my mom.
3. 这个女孩子是谁？
   - Pinyin: Zhè ge nǚ háizi shì shéi?
   - English: Who is this girl?
4. 她是我姐姐。
   - Pinyin: Tā shì wǒ jiějie.
   - English: She is my older sister.
5. 这个男孩子是你弟弟吗？
   - Pinyin: Zhè ge nán háizi shì nǐ dìdi ma?
   - English: Is this boy your younger brother?
6. 不是，他是我大哥的儿子。
   - Pinyin: Bú shì, tā shì wǒ dàgē de érzi.
   - English: No, he is my oldest brother's son.
7. 你大哥有女儿吗？
   - Pinyin: Nǐ dàgē yǒu nǚ'ér ma?
   - English: Does your oldest brother have any daughters?
8. 他没有女儿。
   - Pinyin: Tā méiyǒu nǚ'ér.
   - English: He doesn't have any daughters.

## Dialogue II: Asking About Someone's Family

1. 白英爱，你家有几口人？
   - Pinyin: Bái Yīng'ài, nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?
   - English: Bai Ying'ai, how many people are there in your family?
2. 我家有六口人。我爸爸、我妈妈、一个哥哥、两个妹妹和我。李友，你家有几口人？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ jiā yǒu liù kǒu rén. Wǒ bàba, wǒ māma, yí ge gēge, liǎng ge mèimei hé wǒ. Lǐ Yǒu, nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ kǒu rén?
   - English: There are six people in my family: my dad, my mom, an older brother, two younger sisters and me. Li You, how many people are there in your family?
3. 我家有五口人。爸爸、妈妈、大姐、二姐和我。你爸爸妈妈做什么工作？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ jiā yǒu wǔ kǒu rén. Bàba, māma, dàjiě, èrjiě hé wǒ. Nǐ bàba māma zuò shénme gōngzuò?
   - English: There are five people in my family: my dad, my mom, my oldest sister, my second oldest sister, and me. What do your dad and mom do?
4. 我爸爸是律师，妈妈是英文老师，哥哥、妹妹都是大学生。
   - Pinyin: Wǒ bàba shì lǜshī, māma shì Yīngwén lǎoshī, gēge, mèimei dōu shì dàxuéshēng.
   - English: My dad is a lawyer. My mom is an English teacher. My older brother and younger sisters are all college students.
5. 我妈妈也是老师，我爸爸是医生。
   - Pinyin: Wǒ māma yě shì lǎoshī, wǒ bàba shì yīshēng.
   - English: My mom is also a teacher. My dad is a doctor.

## Tutor Pattern Targets

- Possessive `的`: 你的照片, 我大哥的儿子
- `有 / 没有`: 你大哥有女儿吗？ 他没有女儿。
- Family measure word `口`: 你家有几口人？
- Question pronouns: 谁, 几, 什么
- `都`: 哥哥、妹妹都是大学生。
- Professions: 做什么工作, 律师, 老师, 大学生, 医生
