## Lesson Header

- English title: Dates and Time
- Chinese title: 时间
- Pinyin: Dì sān kè Shíjiān

## Learning Objectives

- Tell and speak about time and dates.
- Talk about one's age and birthday.
- Invite someone to dinner.
- Arrange a dinner date.

## Dialogue I: Taking Someone Out to Eat on His/Her Birthday

1. 白英爱，九月十二号是星期几？
   - Pinyin: Bái Yīng'ài, jiǔyuè shí'èr hào shì xīngqījǐ?
   - English: Bai Ying'ai, what day of the week is September 12?
2. 是星期四。
   - Pinyin: Shì xīngqīsì.
   - English: It's Thursday.
3. 那天是我的生日。
   - Pinyin: Nà tiān shì wǒ de shēngrì.
   - English: That day is my birthday.
4. 是吗？你今年多大？
   - Pinyin: Shì ma? Nǐ jīnnián duō dà?
   - English: Really? How old are you this year?
5. 十八岁。
   - Pinyin: Shíbā suì.
   - English: Eighteen years old.
6. 我星期四请你吃饭，怎么样？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ xīngqīsì qǐng nǐ chī fàn, zěnmeyàng?
   - English: I'll invite you to dinner on Thursday. How does that sound?
7. 太好了。谢谢，谢谢。
   - Pinyin: Tài hǎo le. Xièxie, xièxie.
   - English: Great. Thank you, thank you.
8. 你喜欢吃中国菜还是美国菜？
   - Pinyin: Nǐ xǐhuan chī Zhōngguó cài háishi Měiguó cài?
   - English: Do you like to eat Chinese food or American food?
9. 我是英国人，可是我喜欢吃中国菜。
   - Pinyin: Wǒ shì Yīngguó rén, kěshì wǒ xǐhuan chī Zhōngguó cài.
   - English: I'm British, but I like to eat Chinese food.
10. 好，我们吃中国菜。
   - Pinyin: Hǎo, wǒmen chī Zhōngguó cài.
   - English: OK, we'll eat Chinese food.
11. 星期四几点？
   - Pinyin: Xīngqīsì jǐ diǎn?
   - English: What time on Thursday?
12. 七点半怎么样？
   - Pinyin: Qī diǎn bàn zěnmeyàng?
   - English: How about 7:30?
13. 好，星期四晚上见。
   - Pinyin: Hǎo, xīngqīsì wǎnshang jiàn.
   - English: OK, see you Thursday evening.
14. 再见！
   - Pinyin: Zàijiàn!
   - English: Goodbye!

## Dialogue II: Inviting Someone to Dinner

1. 白英爱，现在几点？
   - Pinyin: Bái Yīng'ài, xiànzài jǐ diǎn?
   - English: Bai Ying'ai, what time is it now?
2. 五点三刻。
   - Pinyin: Wǔ diǎn sān kè.
   - English: Five forty-five.
3. 我六点一刻有事儿。
   - Pinyin: Wǒ liù diǎn yí kè yǒu shìr.
   - English: I have something to do at six fifteen.
4. 你今天很忙，明天忙不忙？
   - Pinyin: Nǐ jīntiān hěn máng, míngtiān máng bu máng?
   - English: You are busy today. Are you busy tomorrow?
5. 我今天很忙，可是明天不忙。有事儿吗？
   - Pinyin: Wǒ jīntiān hěn máng, kěshì míngtiān bù máng. Yǒu shìr ma?
   - English: I'm busy today, but not busy tomorrow. Is something up?
6. 明天我请你吃晚饭，怎么样？
   - Pinyin: Míngtian wǒ qǐng nǐ chī wǎnfàn, zěnmeyàng?
   - English: Tomorrow I'll invite you to dinner. How does that sound?
7. 你为什么请我吃饭？
   - Pinyin: Nǐ wèishénme qǐng wǒ chī fàn?
   - English: Why are you inviting me to dinner?
8. 因为明天是高文中的生日。
   - Pinyin: Yīnwei míngtiān shì Gāo Wénzhōng de shēngrì.
   - English: Because tomorrow is Gao Wenzhong's birthday.
9. 是吗？好。还请谁？
   - Pinyin: Shì ma? Hǎo. Hái qǐng shéi?
   - English: Really? OK. Who else are you inviting?
10. 还请我的同学李友。
   - Pinyin: Hái qǐng wǒ de tóngxué Lǐ Yǒu.
   - English: I'm also inviting my classmate Li You.
11. 那太好了，我认识李友，她也是我的朋友。明天几点？
   - Pinyin: Nà tài hǎo le. Wǒ rènshi Lǐ Yǒu, tā yě shì wǒ de péngyou. Míngtiān jǐ diǎn?
   - English: That's great. I know Li You; she is also my friend. What time tomorrow?
12. 明天晚上七点半。
   - Pinyin: Míngtiān wǎnshang qī diǎn bàn.
   - English: Tomorrow evening at 7:30.
13. 好，明天七点半见。
   - Pinyin: Hǎo, míngtiān qī diǎn bàn jiàn.
   - English: OK, see you tomorrow at 7:30.

## Tutor Pattern Targets

- Dates: 九月十二号是星期几？
- Age and birthday: 你今年多大？ 十八岁。 那天是我的生日。
- Invitation: 我星期四请你吃饭，怎么样？
- Alternative question with `还是`: 中国菜还是美国菜？
- A-not-A question: 明天忙不忙？
- Addition with `还`: 还请谁？ 还请我的同学李友。
